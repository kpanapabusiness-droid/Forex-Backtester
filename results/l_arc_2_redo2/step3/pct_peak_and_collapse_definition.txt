pct_peak_and_collapse — Arc 2 redo2 Step 3 (v2.1.1) definition
================================================================

Source: inherited verbatim from prior arc_2_redo step3 config.
Reference: scripts/arc_2_redo/step3_capturability.py:1140-1144.

Per-trade boolean:
  fwd_mfe_h240 = max(close_r) over full path (baseline R units)
  close_r_at_240 = close_r at bar_offset = 240 (baseline R units)
  peaked     = (fwd_mfe_h240 >= 1.00 R)
  collapsed  = peaked AND (close_r_at_240 <= 0.30 * fwd_mfe_h240)

Cluster aggregate: mean(collapsed) across trades.

v2.1.1 note: the fork's is_held=0 forward bars now carry REAL-MARKET
R-fields (not frozen at final_r as in the prior arc_2_redo). The
conceptual definition is unchanged; the values are now genuine
post-exit price observation over the full 240-bar window rather
than the frozen-at-final-R substitute.

§11 disambiguation thresholds (cfg disambiguation block):
  pct_pc <  0.3 → Early-peak hold
  pct_pc >= 0.5 → Peak-and-collapse
  in between → empirical-defer (flag for chat, do NOT auto-resolve)
